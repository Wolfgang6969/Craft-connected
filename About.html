About Craft Connected

Our Mission

Craft Connected is dedicated to empowering neurodiverse kids through the shared world of Minecraft. Our small-group social sessions are designed to boost confidence, build friendships, and develop communication skills in a fun, supportive environment.

Meet Our Facilitators

Our mentors bring lived experience with ASD and ADHD, combined with training in neurodiverse engagement and online safety. They are passionate about creating a warm and inclusive community where every player feels valued.

NDIS Aligned

Craft Connected sessions are NDIS-funded support activities tailored to meet individual goals, ensuring families get the best value and support.
