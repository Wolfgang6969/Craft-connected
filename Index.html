Craft Connected

Building confidence, connection, and communication — one block at a time.

Craft Connected runs small, supportive Minecraft social groups for neurodiverse kids — proudly funded through NDIS. Sessions are guided by trained mentors with lived experience in ASD/ADHD, creating a safe, fun and empowering space to connect.
