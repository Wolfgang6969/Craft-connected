Program Information

Session Details

  • 1-hour online Minecraft social groups via private Java Edition servers
  • Group size: Up to 5 participants per session
  • Session Times:
    • Weekdays at 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM
    • Saturdays at 12:00 PM, 3:00 PM, and 6:00 PM
  • Age groups: 7–9, 10–12, 13–15, and 16+

What You Need

Participants will need:

  • A computer capable of running Minecraft Java Edition
  • A valid Minecraft Java Edition account
  • Reliable internet connection
  • A quiet space to participate

Our Approach

We focus on creating a safe, inclusive space where neurodiverse kids can build social skills, make friends, and enjoy Minecraft with peers who understand them. Sessions are facilitated by mentors with lived experience and training in neurodiversity support.
